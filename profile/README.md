# Phoenix AI Platform

### AI that completes work.

Phoenix AI Platform is building an AI operating system that transforms natural language into professional work through specialized business plugins.

Rather than replacing existing software, Phoenix orchestrates intelligent workflows that help professionals generate documents, manage operations, analyze infrastructure, and automate repetitive work.

---

## Current Platform

📄 **Phoenix Office**  
AI-powered proposals, CRM, scheduling, documents, and office automation.

⚙️ **Phoenix Core**  
Shared platform services, orchestration, memory, and plugin management.

🧩 **Phoenix SDK**  
Developer SDK for building Phoenix plugins.

📚 **Phoenix Docs**  
Architecture, engineering standards, and platform documentation.

## Vision

Build the world's most capable AI operating system for professionals.

Phoenix transforms natural language into completed work—not just answers.
